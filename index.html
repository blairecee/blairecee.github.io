Our Squad

WE'LL DO RIGHT BY YOUR 'DO

Portia Page

Apprentice

Portia got an early start when she “accidentally” cut off half her hair at 5 years old. While she continued her DIY stylings into her teens to mixed results, she recently redeemed herself at the Canadian Beauty College. Since graduating in 2015, she has discovered a passion for colour. Portia is excited to realize your wildest visions.

Lea Anwar

Expert

Cut Lea and he bleeds Barbicide. Lea grew up surrounded by stylists at the salon his mother ran out of their home. He decided to carry on the family legacy, graduating from La Luma Academy in 2012. There’s nothing more satisfying to Lea than a fresh fade. As a hairstylist by day and a DJ by night, Lea will spin some wild tunes when you take a spin in his barber’s chair.

Sunni Camacho

Master

Sunni discovered a love for esthetics in their teen years. Playing with makeup and fashion opened the door to their passion for hair. After graduating with honours from Avola College of Hairstyling and Esthetics in 2010, Sunni founded Razor’s Edge in 2013. From bantu knots to box braids, Sunni’s mastery of style and eye for beauty will take your hair to the next level.

Razor's Edge

Razor’s Edge Barbershop aims to provide an inclusive space for all people and all hairstyles.

Founded in 2013 by Sunni Camacho, Razor’s Edge has been an iconic part of the Trinity-Bellwoods neighborhood for over seven years. We are proud winners of NOW Magazine’s Best Barbershop of Toronto for three years running.

Come on down, baby, don’t fear the razor.
